Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix? +
Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices. You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want without a single commercial – all for one low monthly price.
How much does Netflix cost? +
Netflix offers several plans to meet your needs. The basic plan starts at $8.99 per month, the standard plan (which includes HD) costs $13.99 per month, and the premium plan (which includes Ultra HD) is $17.99 per month. Prices may vary slightly depending on your location.
Where can I watch? +
You can watch Netflix through any internet-connected device that offers the Netflix app, including smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, smartphones, tablets, and computers. You can also watch Netflix on your computer using an internet browser.
How do I cancel? +
Netflix is flexible. There are no pesky contracts and no commitments. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime.
What can I watch on Netflix? +
Netflix has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, award-winning Netflix originals, and more. Watch as much as you want, anytime you want.
Is Netflix good for kids? +
The Netflix Kids experience is included in your membership to give parents control while kids enjoy family-friendly TV shows and movies in their own space. Kids profiles come with PIN-protected parental controls to prevent children from accessing inappropriate content.
